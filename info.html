What is Oil Co. ?

Oil Co. is a premier oil and gas company located in Iraq, focused on delivering high-quality energy solutions while prioritizing innovation and sustainability. We leverage Iraq's abundant natural resources to ensure reliable oil production and distribution. Our commitment extends beyond just meeting energy demands; we strive to implement environmentally friendly practices and invest in community development. By integrating advanced technologies and sustainable practices, we aim to enhance efficiency and minimize our ecological footprint. Our experienced team is dedicated to maintaining safety and operational excellence, ensuring that we not only support the energy needs of today but also contribute to a sustainable future.

As a leading oil company in Iraq, we are dedicated to harnessing the country's rich energy resources to fuel sustainable growth and development. With a commitment to innovation and excellence, we operate within the highest environmental and safety standards, ensuring responsible extraction and production processes. Our skilled workforce, combined with advanced technology, enables us to efficiently tap into Iraq's vast oil reserves while contributing to local communities through job creation and infrastructure development. We strive to be a trusted partner in Iraq's energy sector, driving economic prosperity and enhancing energy security for the future
