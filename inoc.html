Iraq National Oil Company

Overview of INOC

The Iraq National Oil Company (INOC) plays a pivotal role in the country's economy and energy sector. Established in 1966, INOC is responsible for the exploration, production, and management of oil resources in Iraq. As one of the largest oil producers globally, INOC aims to enhance oil production capacities while adhering to international standards and practices. Its mission is to ensure sustainable growth and development of the oil industry in Iraq, contributing significantly to the nation's GDP and infrastructure.

Oil Production and Reserves

Iraq is endowed with one of the largest proven oil reserves in the world, with INOC at the forefront of its extraction and management. The company is actively engaged in modernizing its production techniques to maximize output and efficiency. With major oil fields like Rumaila, West Qurna, and Majnoon under its management, INOC is committed to increasing production levels while minimizing environmental impact. The strategic development of these fields is crucial for meeting both domestic and international energy demands

Partnerships and Collaborations

INOC recognizes the importance of collaboration to enhance its operational capabilities. The company has forged strategic partnerships with leading international oil companies to leverage technology and expertise. These collaborations focus on upstream activities, including exploration and production, as well as downstream operations such as refining and distribution. By working together with global partners, INOC aims to boost efficiency, enhance workforce skills, and improve oil recovery rates, ensuring a competitive edge in the global market.

Commitment to Sustainability

As part of its commitment to sustainability, INOC is actively exploring renewable energy initiatives and environmentally friendly practices. The company understands the global shift towards cleaner energy and is investing in technologies that reduce carbon emissions and environmental footprints. INOC's strategy includes the integration of sustainable practices in oil extraction and production, as well as exploring alternative energy sources, ensuring that Iraq's oil wealth contributes to both economic prosperity and environmental stewardship for future generations.
