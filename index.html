North Oil Company (NOC)

North Oil Company was established in 1972 and is one of the key state-owned enterprises in Iraq. It manages oil extraction and refining operations primarily in northern Iraq, focusing on the Kirkuk oil fields and surrounding areas.

Iraq National Oil Company (INOC)

The Iraq National Oil Company was founded in 1964, with the primary aim of organizing and managing the oil industry in Iraq. The company has undergone several restructurings, especially after 2003, to adapt to the political and economic changes in the country.
